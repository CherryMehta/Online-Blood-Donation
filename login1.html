Facts About Blood Donation

  • One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Your body replaces the lost blood quickly. Plasma within 24 hours and RBCs in about 4 to 6 weeks.
  • O-negative is the universal donor type.
  • Regular donations help keep blood supplies fresh.
  • Donating blood can have health benefits like iron regulation, calorie burn etc.
  • Most eligible people do not donate regularly. Less than 10% of eligible people donate regularly, despite the constant demand.
